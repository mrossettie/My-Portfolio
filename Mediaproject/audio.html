Radiohead - Exit Music (For A Film)

"Exit Music (For a Film)" by Radiohead is a song composed for the end credits of the 1996 film Romeo + Juliet. The song is a narrative summary of the lovers' tragic story, portraying their desperation to escape their feuding families and the ultimate finality of their deaths as a form of everlasting peace.

Enjambre - Impacto

The song Impacto explores the intense emotional and physical impact another person's presence and gaze has on the singer. The lyrics describe feeling intimidated and overwhelmed, but also vulnerable and smitten.
